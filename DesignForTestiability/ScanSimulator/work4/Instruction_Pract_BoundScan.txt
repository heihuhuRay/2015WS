Usage of ScanSim.exe and ScanEducator:
*****************************************

1.Open DosBox  (Click on brown desktop symbol)

2. Write the following lines in the DosBox terminal:
> mount E E:\work4
> e:
> scansim.exe

Close with ALT+Tab and close DosBox-program

3. Call "Scaned":
Write the following lines in the DosBox terminal:
> mount E E:\work4
> e:
> scaned

Close with ALT+Tab and close DosBox-program
